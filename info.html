Transformers - Movie Info

Release Date: July 3, 2007

Director: Michael Bay

Main Cast: Shia LaBeouf, Megan Fox, Josh Duhamel

Genre: Action, Sci-Fi, Adventure

Plot Summary

Transformers is about a battle between two alien robot factions, the Autobots and the Decepticons, who bring their war to Earth. A young man named Sam Witwicky becomes caught in the middle as the Autobots fight to protect humanity while the Decepticons seek ultimate power.

Fun Facts

  • The movie is based on the popular Hasbro toy line and cartoon series.
  • It grossed over $700 million worldwide.
  • The franchise later expanded into multiple sequels and spin-offs.
